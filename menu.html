Our Menu

At Wildflour Café, we believe everyone deserves to enjoy delicious baked goods and drinks without compromise. That’s why all of our offerings are made 100% gluten free.

We carefully select alternative flours such as almond, rice, and oat, and we use natural sweeteners and plant-based milks when possible. Many items can be customized to suit dietary preferences — whether you’d like a dairy-free latte or a nut-free dessert option, we’re happy to provide substitutions.
GF = Gluten Free, DF = Dairy Free
