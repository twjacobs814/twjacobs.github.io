Our Story

Wildflour Café was born from a simple idea: everyone deserves treats that feel special. We started baking gluten-free desserts for friends and family, and that passion quickly grew into a cozy neighborhood café.

Today we craft everything in small batches with care—using alternative flours, simple ingredients, and a lot of heart. Whether you’re here for a quiet latte or a celebratory slice, we’re glad you’re part of our story.

We’re committed to sustainability, community, and making delicious gluten-free treats that everyone can enjoy.
